When will I hear from my Breastpump Specialist?

Once your Breastpump Specialist has finished verifying your benefits he or she will reach out to you either by phone or email, so keep an eye out for us! Typically this takes between 7 and 10 business days.

When will I receive my breastpump?

Shipping time frame varies by insurance. Some insurance companies allow you to receive a breastpump any time during your pregnancy while others require we wait until after the birth to ship your breastpump. Your Breastpump Specialist will explain your individual benefits.

What type of pump am I eligible for?

Eligible pumps vary by insurance plan. Once we verify your insurance benefits your Breastpump Specialist will contact you to review all the pumps you are eligible for.
